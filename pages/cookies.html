Cookie Policy

Learn how SkillSync AI uses cookies to enhance your experience and protect your privacy

Last updated: January 15, 2024
Important Notice
By continuing to use SkillSync AI, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and improving our services.

SkillSync AI uses cookies to enhance functionality, analyze website traffic, and personalize content. We are committed to being transparent about how we collect and use this information.

Your Privacy Matters
We respect your privacy and ensure that cookies are used responsibly. You have full control over which cookies you accept, and we'll explain exactly what each type does.

Types of Cookies We Use

SkillSync AI uses different types of cookies, each serving a specific purpose to improve your experience:

Essential Cookies
These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic features like security, network management, and accessibility.
Examples:
Session management, authentication, security tokens, load balancing
Analytics Cookies
These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information about usage patterns and performance.
Examples:
Google Analytics, page views, bounce rate, traffic sources, user flows
Functional Cookies
These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization. They remember your choices and preferences to provide a more customized experience.
Examples:
Language preferences, theme settings, dashboard layout, form data
Marketing Cookies
These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns across different platforms.
Examples:
Ad targeting, conversion tracking, social media pixels, remarketing

How We Use Cookies

SkillSync AI uses cookies to provide and improve our services in the following ways:

  • Authentication & Security: To keep you logged in securely and protect against unauthorized access
  • Personalization: To remember your preferences, settings, and customize your dashboard experience
  • Performance Analysis: To understand how users interact with our platform and identify areas for improvement
  • Content Optimization: To deliver relevant job matches and recommendations based on your profile
  • Communication: To provide timely notifications about matches, messages, and platform updates
  • Error Prevention: To identify and fix technical issues, ensuring a smooth user experience
  • Legal Compliance: To maintain records required by law and ensure platform security
Data Protection
All cookie data is encrypted and stored securely. We never sell your personal information to third parties, and we only share anonymized analytics data to improve our services.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have complete control over how cookies are used on your device. Here are several ways to manage your cookie preferences:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser settings to decline cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Site permissions > Cookies and site data

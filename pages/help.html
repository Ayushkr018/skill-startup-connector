Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a strong profile on SkillSync AI?

To create a strong profile: 1) Add a professional photo 2) Write a compelling summary highlighting your skills and experience 3) List all relevant skills and technologies 4) Include your work experience with specific achievements 5) Add your education and certifications 6) Keep your profile updated regularly.

How does the job matching algorithm work?

Our AI algorithm analyzes your skills, experience, location preferences, and career goals to match you with relevant job opportunities. The match percentage is calculated based on how well your profile aligns with job requirements. Higher match percentages indicate better fit.

What should I do if I'm not getting good matches?

If you're not getting good matches: 1) Update your skills section with in-demand technologies 2) Optimize your job preferences and location settings 3) Add more detail to your experience section 4) Take skill assessments to validate your abilities 5) Make sure your profile is 100% complete.

How do I communicate with recruiters?

You can communicate with recruiters through our messaging system. When a recruiter shows interest in your profile, you'll receive a notification. Be professional, respond promptly, and ask relevant questions about the role and company.

Are there any fees for using SkillSync AI?

SkillSync AI is completely free for job seekers. You can create a profile, receive job matches, communicate with recruiters, and access all our tools without any cost. We make money by helping companies find the right talent.

How do I prepare for interviews?

Use our Interview Prep tool to practice common questions, take mock interviews with our AI, research the company thoroughly, prepare specific examples using the STAR method, and practice both technical and behavioral questions relevant to your field.

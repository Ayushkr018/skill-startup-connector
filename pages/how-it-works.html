‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about how SkillSync AI works

How accurate is the AI matching system?

Our AI matching system has a 95% accuracy rate based on user feedback and successful placements. The algorithm continuously learns and improves from each interaction, considering factors like skills compatibility, cultural fit, career goals, and growth potential.

Is SkillSync AI free for students?

Yes! SkillSync AI is completely free for students. We believe in democratizing access to opportunities, so students can create profiles, take assessments, connect with startups, and access all platform features at no cost.

How long does the matching process take?

Once you complete your profile, initial matches typically appear within 24 hours. The AI continuously analyzes new opportunities and profiles, so you may receive new matches daily. The entire process from signup to job offer averages 2-3 weeks.

What types of startups are on the platform?

We partner with startups across various industries including tech, fintech, healthcare, e-commerce, AI/ML, blockchain, and more. Our startup partners range from early-stage companies to well-funded scale-ups, all committed to hiring and developing young talent.

Can I work remotely through SkillSync AI?

Absolutely! Many of our startup partners offer remote, hybrid, and flexible work arrangements. You can specify your location preferences during profile creation, and our matching algorithm will prioritize opportunities that align with your preferred work style.

What support do you provide after matching?

We provide comprehensive post-match support including interview preparation, salary negotiation guidance, onboarding assistance, mentorship programs, and ongoing career development resources. Our success team stays involved throughout your journey.

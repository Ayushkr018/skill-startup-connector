🚀 About SkillSync AI

SkillSync AI is revolutionizing talent discovery through artificial intelligence, connecting talented students with innovative startups worldwide. Founded in 2024, our platform uses advanced machine learning algorithms to create perfect matches based on skills, culture fit, and career aspirations.

With over 10,000+ students and 500+ partner startups, we've achieved an industry-leading 85% success rate in talent matching. Our mission is to democratize opportunities and build the future workforce through intelligent technology.

Based in India with a global vision, SkillSync AI is backed by leading investors and has been recognized as a breakthrough innovation in HR technology and AI-powered recruitment.

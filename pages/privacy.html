Information We Collect

At SkillSync AI, we collect information to provide you with the best AI-powered skill development experience. We are committed to transparency about what data we collect and how we use it.

🔐 Personal Information

  • Full name and email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Professional title and experience level
  • Educational background
  • Career goals and interests

📊 Learning Data

  • Skill assessments and progress
  • Learning preferences and patterns
  • Course completions and certifications
  • AI interaction history
  • Performance analytics

💻 Technical Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • Browser information and version
  • IP address and location data
  • Usage analytics and session data
  • Error logs and performance metrics

Data Collection Principles

  • Minimal Collection: We only collect data necessary for our services
  • Purpose Limitation: Data is used only for specified, legitimate purposes
  • Transparency: Clear communication about what we collect and why
  • User Control: You can manage your data preferences at any time

How We Use Your Information

Your data powers our AI-driven personalization engine to create the most effective learning experience tailored specifically for you.

AI Personalization

We use your learning data and preferences to train our AI algorithms, providing personalized course recommendations, skill gap analysis, and adaptive learning paths.

Progress Tracking

Your performance data helps us track your learning progress, identify areas for improvement, and celebrate your achievements.

Communication

We use your contact information to send important updates, learning reminders, and promotional content (which you can opt out of).

Service Improvement

Aggregated, anonymized data helps us improve our platform, develop new features, and enhance the overall user experience.

Information Sharing

We believe in protecting your privacy while providing exceptional service. Here's how and when we share your information:

When We Share Information

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party services that help us operate our platform
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale
  • Consent: When you explicitly consent to sharing with specific parties

When We DON'T Share Information

  • ❌ We never sell your personal data to advertisers
  • ❌ We don't share individual learning data with employers without consent
  • ❌ We don't provide personal information to marketing companies
  • ❌ We don't share sensitive data for commercial purposes

Data Sharing Safeguards

  • All third-party partners must sign strict data protection agreements
  • Regular audits ensure compliance with our privacy standards
  • Data minimization - partners only receive necessary information
  • You can request details about any data sharing arrangements

Data Security

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256 encryption standards.

Access Controls

Strict role-based access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access your data.

Regular Audits

Our security practices undergo regular third-party audits and penetration testing.

Incident Response

We have a comprehensive incident response plan and will notify you of any security breaches.

Security Certifications & Compliance

  • SOC 2 Type II certified
  • GDPR compliant data processing
  • ISO 27001 security standards
  • Regular vulnerability assessments
  • 24/7 security monitoring

Your Privacy Rights

You have comprehensive control over your personal data. We respect your privacy rights and make it easy to exercise them.

Right to Access

Request a copy of all personal data we have about you in a portable format.

Right to Rectification

Correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal information we hold about you.

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data when it's no longer necessary or lawful.

Right to Object

Object to processing of your personal data for marketing or other purposes.

Data Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format to transfer to another service.

Restrict Processing

Limit how we use your personal data while maintaining your account.

Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at:

Email: privacy@skillsync.ai

Response Time: We'll respond within 30 days

Verification: We may need to verify your identity to process requests

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage patterns, and provide personalized content.

Types of Cookies We Use

🍪 Essential Cookies

  • Authentication and security
  • Session management
  • Load balancing
  • Basic functionality

📈 Analytics Cookies

  • Usage statistics
  • Performance monitoring
  • Feature usage tracking
  • Error reporting

🎯 Personalization Cookies

  • Language settings
  • Customized content
  • Learning recommendations

Cookie Management

You can control cookie settings through:

  • Browser settings and preferences
  • Our cookie preference center
  • Account privacy settings
  • Third-party opt-out tools

Third-Party Services

We work with trusted third-party service providers to deliver our services. Here's how we manage these relationships:

Service Categories

  • Cloud Hosting: AWS, Google Cloud for secure data storage
  • Analytics: Google Analytics for usage insights
  • Communication: SendGrid for email delivery
  • Payment Processing: Stripe for secure transactions
  • Customer Support: Intercom for help desk services

Third-Party Data Protection

  • All partners undergo thorough security assessments
  • Data processing agreements ensure GDPR compliance
  • Regular audits verify adherence to privacy standards
  • Minimal data sharing - only what's necessary for service delivery

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

Retention Periods

  • Account Data: Until account deletion + 30 days
  • Learning Records: 7 years for certification purposes
  • Payment Information: 7 years for tax/legal requirements
  • Marketing Data: Until opt-out + 30 days
  • Support Tickets: 3 years for quality assurance

Automated Deletion

  • Automated systems delete expired data
  • Regular audits ensure compliance
  • Secure deletion protocols protect against recovery
  • You can request early deletion of non-essential data

International Data Transfers

SkillSync AI operates globally. When we transfer your data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

Transfer Mechanisms

  • Adequacy Decisions: Transfers to countries with adequate protection
  • Standard Contractual Clauses: EU-approved data transfer agreements
  • Binding Corporate Rules: Internal policies for multinational transfers
  • Certification Schemes: Privacy Shield successors and equivalents

Data Processing Locations

Primary: United States (AWS US-East)

Secondary: European Union (AWS Frankfurt)

Backup: Canada (AWS Canada-Central)

Children's Privacy

SkillSync AI is designed for professional development and is not intended for children under 16 years of age.

Age Restrictions

  • Minimum Age: 16 years old
  • Verification: Age confirmation during registration
  • Parental Consent: Required for users 16-18 in some jurisdictions
  • Account Termination: Immediate deletion if underage use discovered

If You're Under 16

  • Do not create an account or provide personal information
  • Ask a parent or guardian to contact us if interested in our services
  • We will delete any accounts created by users under 16
  • Consider age-appropriate educational resources instead

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We'll notify you of significant changes and give you time to review them.

Change Notification Process

  • Email Notification: 30 days before major changes take effect
  • In-App Notices: Prominent notifications in your dashboard
  • Website Banner: Clear notice on our homepage
  • Version History: Track all changes in our policy archive

Your Options

  • Review changes during the 30-day notice period
  • Contact us with questions or concerns
  • Delete your account if you don't agree with changes
  • Continue using our services indicates acceptance

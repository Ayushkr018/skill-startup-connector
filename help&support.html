Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about SkillSync AI

How do I create my first job posting?
To create your first job posting, navigate to the "Jobs" section in your dashboard, click "Post New Job", fill in the job details including title, description, requirements, and location. Our AI will automatically suggest optimal posting settings based on your requirements.
How does the AI matching system work?
Our AI matching system analyzes candidate profiles, skills, experience, and preferences against your job requirements. It uses machine learning algorithms to provide accuracy scores and match percentages, helping you find the most suitable candidates efficiently.
Can I integrate SkillSync AI with other tools?
Yes! SkillSync AI offers integrations with popular tools like Slack, Google Calendar, LinkedIn, and many ATS systems. You can find all available integrations in the Settings > Integrations section of your dashboard.
What support do you offer for team collaboration?
SkillSync AI provides comprehensive team collaboration features including role-based permissions, shared candidate pools, collaborative feedback systems, and real-time notifications. Team members can work together seamlessly on hiring projects.
How secure is my data with SkillSync AI?
We take data security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, we comply with GDPR and SOC 2 standards, and we never share your data with third parties. Regular security audits ensure your information remains protected.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?
Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from the Billing section. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades will be applied at the next billing cycle. No hidden fees or long-term commitments.

About Us

TechStart Inc. is an innovative HR-tech startup headquartered in Bengaluru, India. Our mission is to democratize access to intelligent hiring by providing AI-driven candidate matching, assessment, and pipeline analytics for early-stage and growth-stage companies alike.

Within three years, we’ve partnered with 120+ startups worldwide, reduced their average time-to-hire by 42 %, and secured a 4.8★ customer satisfaction rating. Backed by leading VCs, we are now scaling our platform and expanding into new markets across Southeast Asia and the US.
